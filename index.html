I worked in this position for the three years that I attended PPU, even as I took on more roles in the Point Park community.

In my sophmore year at Point Park I had the opportunity to serve as a Pioneer Ambassador, essentially a school ambassador. As a PA I helped to greet new students and families joining the Point Park communitty, gave tours to help students become family with the campus and downtown communitty, and gave presentations to large groups to educate them on the Point Park experience. I worked in this role for a year.

In my junior year at Point Park, I worked as a Resident Educator, essentially an RA.
